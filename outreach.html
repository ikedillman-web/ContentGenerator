How this tool uses the API

• Venue Research — Claude searches for real nightclubs, bars, and lounges in your target city and returns venue details, vibe descriptions, and likely music formats.

• Outreach Email — Claude generates a personalized pitch for each venue, matching your DJ profile to their specific vibe and format — hip-hop heavy venues get a different pitch than EDM clubs.

• Cost — Each venue search + email generation uses roughly 2,000–3,000 tokens (~$0.01–0.02 at current Claude Sonnet pricing). Very affordable.
